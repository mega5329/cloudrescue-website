Benefits of Sponsoring a Dog

Your sponsorship makes a real difference

Special Treatment & Care

Shelters can provide treats and special treatment to your sponsored dog, ensuring they receive extra love and attention.

Send Requests to Shelter

You can send requests to the shelter manager about your sponsored dog. Want to see them playing? Need an update on their health? Just ask!

Direct Communication

Shelter managers will respond to your messages through the in-app chat, keeping you connected and informed about your sponsored dog's well-being.

Weekly Video Updates

Receive mandatory weekly video updates showing your sponsored dog's progress, activities, and daily life at the shelter.

What's Included:

  • Weekly video updates from the shelter
  • Direct communication with the shelter manager
  • Send requests to the shelter for special care or updates
  • Shelter provides treats and special treatment to your sponsored dog
  • Tax-deductible donation receipt
  • All funds go directly to the shelter
