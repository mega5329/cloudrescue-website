About Cloud Rescue Foundation

Connecting hearts across borders, one rescue dog at a time

Our Mission

To revolutionize animal welfare by creating a transparent, sustainable bridge between compassionate people worldwide and rescue dogs in China through innovative cloud adoption technology.

Our Story

Cloud Rescue Foundation was born from a simple observation: countless people want to help rescue dogs but face barriers like distance, long-term commitment requirements, and lack of transparency. Meanwhile, rescue shelters in China struggle with chronic funding shortages and lack of international connections.

We recognized that technology could bridge these gaps. By creating a platform that enables "cloud adoption," we allow people worldwide to make meaningful weekly contributions to dogs they care about, while maintaining complete transparency through video updates and direct communication with shelter managers.

Today, we're proud to connect thousands of adopters with verified shelters across China, providing sustainable support for dogs who need it most while empowering shelters to grow responsibly.

Our Core Values

Compassion

Every dog deserves love, care, and a chance at a better life.

Transparency

Innovation

Using technology to solve old problems in new ways.

Community
