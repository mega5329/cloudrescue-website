The Challenge

Rescue shelters face critical barriers that prevent dogs from finding homes

Limited Shelter Resources

Rescue shelters in China face chronic funding shortages, making it difficult to care for all dogs in need.

High Adoption Barriers

Traditional adoption requires long-term commitment many can't make, leaving countless dogs without support.

Distance Barrier

People abroad want to help but lack direct connection to make a meaningful impact.
