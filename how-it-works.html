How Cloud Rescue Works

Simple steps to make a big impact

For Adopters

1. Download the App

Get Cloud Rescue on iOS or Android and create your account.

2. Browse and Choose

Browse verified shelters and select a random or specific dog to sponsor.

3. Receive Updates

Get bi-weekly video updates and chat directly with shelter managers.

For Shelters

1. Apply and Get Verified

Submit your shelter application and undergo our verification process.

2. Create Dog Profiles

Add your rescue dogs with photos and videos.

3. Receive Support

Get weekly donations and grow your capacity sustainably.
