Installation Instructions

1

Download the APK

Click the download button above to download the Cloud Rescue APK file to your Android device.

2

Enable Unknown Sources

Before installing, you need to allow installation from unknown sources. Go to Settings > Security > Enable 'Install unknown apps' or 'Unknown sources'.

3

Trust the APK

When you open the downloaded APK file, your device may show a security warning. Tap 'Install' or 'Allow' to proceed. This is safe - the app is verified by Cloud Rescue Foundation.

4

Complete Installation

Follow the on-screen prompts to complete the installation. Once installed, open the app and start sponsoring rescue dogs!

Important Notes

This APK is only for Android devices. iOS users should download from the App Store.

You may need to allow installation from 'Unknown Sources' in your device settings.

The first time you install, your device may warn you about installing from an unknown source. This is normal - tap 'Install anyway' or 'Allow'.

If you encounter any issues, please contact us at admin@cloudrescuefoundation.org

Is it Safe?

Yes! This APK is the official Cloud Rescue app. We are a verified nonprofit organization. The app is safe to install and use.
