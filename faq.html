General Questions

What is Cloud Rescue Foundation?

▼

Cloud Rescue Foundation is a platform connecting people worldwide with rescue dogs in China through "cloud adoption." Instead of traditional adoption, you sponsor a dog remotely with weekly donations and receive regular updates.

How much does it cost?

▼

Random dog: $9.99/week. Specific dog: $59.99/5 weeks. Pricing varies by size: Small $9.99, Medium $15.99, Large $20.99 per week.

Can I cancel anytime?

▼

Yes, you can cancel your cloud adoption at any time with no penalties or fees.

How often will I get updates?

▼

Shelters are required to provide bi-weekly video updates. If they miss updates, the dog becomes ineligible for new adoptions.

Is this legitimate?

▼

Yes! Every shelter undergoes manual verification by our customer service team. We provide complete transparency with transaction history and regular video updates.
