5. Cloud Adoption Services

5.1 How Cloud Adoption Works

Cloud Adoption allows you to remotely sponsor rescue dogs through recurring donations:

  • Random Dog: $9.90/week (matched with a dog in need)
  • Specific Dog: $59.99 for 5 weeks (choose your dog)
  • Size-Based: Small dogs $9.99/week, Medium $15.99/week, Large $20.99/week
  • Renewal: Flexible 1-20 weeks at your dog's size rate
  • Donations support food, medical care, and shelter operations

5.2 Donations and Payments

All donations are voluntary and non-refundable unless required by law. By making a donation:

  • You authorize recurring charges (for weekly subscriptions)
  • You understand donations go directly to shelter operations
  • You acknowledge Cloud Rescue Foundation retains a small administrative fee to maintain platform operations
  • You agree to provide valid payment information

5.3 Video Updates

Shelters are required to provide weekly video updates of sponsored dogs. If a shelter fails to provide updates for two consecutive weeks, the dog becomes ineligible for cloud adoption until updates resume. We will notify you and offer to reassign your sponsorship.
